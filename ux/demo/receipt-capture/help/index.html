Recover Your Depreciation

After replacing your lost or damaged item(s), you may submit your replacement receipt(s) to collect the depreciation withheld from your initial payment up to the replacement cost determined for the item.

Add Receipts for Replaced Items

Locate the item(s) that you have replaced and click Add. Follow the steps to upload your receipt image and enter the receipt details including cost and quantity for the item(s) you have replaced. Highlight the item on the receipt image. Repeat this process for all the items you have replaced.

Submit Receipts

Submit your receipts for review by clicking Submit Receipts. They will be evaluated for recovery of depreciation available to you, as outlined by your policy.
